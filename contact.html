Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I prepare before our consultation?

To make our initial consultation productive, it helps to have a general idea of your project goals, target audience, desired features, and any design preferences. If you have examples of websites or designs you like, those are helpful too. For database projects, information about your current data structure is valuable.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please indicate the urgency in your message, and I'll do my best to get back to you sooner.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients from around the world. I'm comfortable communicating via email, video calls, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of time zones and geographical locations.

What happens after I submit this contact form?

After you submit the form, I'll review your project details and reach out to schedule an initial consultation. During this call, we'll discuss your project in more depth, answer any questions you might have, and determine if we're a good fit to work together. Following that, I'll prepare a detailed proposal outlining the project scope, timeline, and cost.
