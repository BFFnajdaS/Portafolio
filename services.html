Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline varies depending on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months or more. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing depending on the nature of the work. For most website and database projects, I provide a fixed project quote after understanding your requirements. Maintenance and support services are typically offered as monthly packages.

Do you provide content for websites?

I focus on design and development rather than content creation. However, I can provide guidance on content structure and recommend professional copywriters if needed. I'll work with you to integrate your content effectively into the website design.

Can you help migrate my existing database?

Yes, I provide data migration services to help you transition from legacy systems to more modern and efficient database solutions. This includes data extraction, transformation, and loading processes to ensure your information is accurately transferred and optimized in the new system.

Do you offer website hosting?

While I don't provide hosting services directly, I can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your project requirements and help set up your website on your chosen hosting platform. I can also assist with domain registration and configuration if needed.

What happens after my website is launched?

After launch, I offer various maintenance and support packages to ensure your website continues to function optimally. This includes security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. I'll work with you to determine the level of ongoing support that best meets your needs and budget.
